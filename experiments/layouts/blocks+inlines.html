You will see that when you click on the first paragraph and drag the mouse in the y axis, you are getting feedback to show that you are dragging the first paragraph; however, the position value of the paragraph is affected only until the mouse button is released. Then the paragraph is removed from the DOM and re-appended after this paragraph and its position value is returned to what it was before the drag procedure.
